This is, Vision & Mission

Introduction:

  • Established in 1965, the Computer Society of India (CSI) has played a pivotal role in shaping and steering the Indian Information Technology (IT) industry since its inception.


Impactful Presence:

  • Over the years, CSI has expanded its footprint, currently boasting a vast network comprising 73 chapters across India. This extensive reach includes over 500 student branches, making it a vibrant community of technology enthusiasts. Moreover, CSI proudly encompasses a membership exceeding 100,000 individuals, counting among them India's most renowned IT industry leaders, accomplished scientists, and dedicated academicians.


Our Mission:

  • CSI is dedicated to fostering research, facilitating knowledge sharing, promoting continuous learning, and enhancing career opportunities for IT professionals across all categories. Simultaneously, we are committed to inspiring and nurturing newcomers in the industry, helping them seamlessly integrate into the dynamic IT community. We collaborate closely with industry associations, government bodies, and academic institutions to ensure that the transformative benefits of IT advancement are accessible to every citizen of India.

What We Do

Technical Workshops and Seminars

Organize and conduct workshops and seminars on emerging technologies, software dev, and other CS-related topics. These events help students stay updated with the latest trends in the industry.

Guest Lectures and Expert Talks

Invite industry experts, researchers, and professionals to deliver lectures and talks on various subjects related to computer science and technology, offering valuable insights to students.

Skill Development Programs

Provide training sessions and workshops to enhance students' technical skills, such as programming, web development, and database management.

Hackathons and Coding Competitions

Arrange coding competitions, hackathons, and programming contests to foster a competitive spirit among students and enhance their problem-solving skills.

Project and Research Support

Assist students in finding project ideas, mentors, and resources for research projects and practical applications of computer science concepts.

Networking and Industry Connections

Establish connections with IT companies and organizations to facilitate internships, job placements, and industry exposure for students.
